[i]From the founding of our civilization, our cities ships have always looked far more sophisticated than our age would suggest. When we discovered just how similar our stylish designs are with the Precursor Fallen Empires however, we learned that perhaps there are things our ancestors hid in the history books...[/i]

This mod is an add-on component mod to [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2044599287]The Zenith of Fallen Empires 3.0[/url] and provides the functionality of starting with Fallen Empire ship sets, Fallen Empire City sets, and Fallen Empire Diplomatic Rooms. Note that this mod requires the base mod to run and no new ship classes are added.



[h1]Add-on Features:[/h1]

[list]

[*][b]Ability to start with 1 of the 16 Fallen Empire ship set color variants[/b]

[*][b]Ability to start with 1 of the 16 Fallen Empire city sets[/b]

[*][b]Fallen Empire Diplomacy Rooms[/b]

[/list]
